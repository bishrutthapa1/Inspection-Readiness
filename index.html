What is Inspection Readiness?

Inspection readiness is the continuous state of operational control, documentation integrity, and personnel preparedness that enables an organization to successfully host regulatory inspections at any time.

Inspection Do’s & Don’ts — CALM SAFE

CALM SAFE
  • C – Communicate clearly
  • A – Answer only the question
  • L – Listen carefully
  • M – Maintain professionalism
  • S – Show data, not opinion
  • A – Ask for clarification
  • F – Follow SOPs
  • E – Escalate when needed

SMART CAPA

  • S – Specific
  • M – Measurable
  • A – Achievable
  • R – Root cause based
  • T – Timely

A strong CAPA includes root cause analysis, corrective and preventive separation, ownership, deadlines, and effectiveness checks.
